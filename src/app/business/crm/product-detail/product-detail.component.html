Features

At maturity UAP Old Mutual guarantees that the benefit payable will not be less than the total contributions received during the term of the policy less charges and withdrawals

The policy allows you to make lump sum injections worth at least three times your monthly contribution

The policy has flexible premium payment plans. Premiums can be paid monthly, quarterly or annually

Policy alterations such as premium frequency change, premium increase or decrease are allowed

The policy can be used as collateral to secure loans
